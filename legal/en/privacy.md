## 5. Use of Photographs

Photographs of your property are collected and used exclusively with your **explicit consent**, granted through the onboarding form at the time of service activation.

Said photographs may be published on:
- The property portal at [www.casaconcierge.co](https://www.casaconcierge.co/)
- Listings on rental platforms (Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com)
- Marketing materials related to your property

**Upon cancellation of your subscription**, your photographs will be removed from the Casa Concierge PDC public portal within a maximum period of **48 hours**. You will receive a confirmation email when this occurs.

You may request the removal of your photographs at any time, even without canceling the service, by sending a request to [casaconciergepdc@outlook.com].

## 6. ARCO Rights (Access, Rectification, Cancellation, Opposition)

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Know what personal data we hold about you.
- **Rectification:** Request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Cancellation:** Request the deletion of your data when it is no longer necessary.
- **Opposition:** Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.

**Users residing in the European Union:** Additionally, and in accordance with the GDPR, you have the right to data portability and to file a complaint with the supervisory authority of your country.

To exercise any of these rights, send a request to:

📧 **[casaconciergepdc@outlook.com]**

Your request must include: full name, registered email address, a clear description of the right you wish to exercise, and a copy of official identification. We will respond within a maximum period of **20 business days**.

## 7. Legal Consent Records

When you accept our Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Cookie Policy at the time of subscription, we automatically record:

- Exact version of each document accepted
- Date and time of acceptance (UTC)
- IP address of the device used

These records are retained permanently as evidence of the contractual relationship, even after the cancellation of the service. They cannot be deleted through an ARCO request, as they constitute a legal and audit requirement.

## 8. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- HTTPS encrypted connection across the entire site
- Role-based restricted data access (authorized personnel only)

## 9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as you maintain an active relationship with us. Once you cancel your subscription:

- Legal consent records are retained indefinitely.
- You may request early deletion of operational data through an ARCO request.
