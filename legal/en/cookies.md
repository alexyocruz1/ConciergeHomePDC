## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit it. They allow the site to remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language, whether you are already logged in, or how you interacted with the site.

---

## 2. Why Do We Use Cookies?

Casa Concierge PDC uses cookies to:

- Keep your session active on the owner portal
- Remember your language preferences
- Analyze how visitors use the site in order to improve it
- Measure the performance of our pages
- Ensure the security of your account

## 4. Types of Cookies We Use

### 4.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cookies are essential for the site to function properly. Without them, you will not be able to log in or navigate the owner portal.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| `session_token` | Keeps your session active | Until logout |
| `csrf_token` | Protects against security attacks | Per session |
| `locale` | Remembers your selected language | 12 months |
| `cookie_consent` | Stores your cookie preferences | 12 months |

These cookies **cannot be disabled** as they are necessary for the service to function.

## 5. How We Obtain Your Consent

When you visit our site for the first time, you will see a **cookie consent banner** with the following options, clearly visible and of equal visual weight:

```
[ Reject All ]   [ Customize ]   [ Accept All ]
```

**Banner rules:**
- No button is pre-selected
- "Reject All" and "Accept All" are the same size and visual style — we do not manipulate the design to favor any option
- The banner does not disappear automatically — it requires a deliberate action from the user
- If you close the banner without making a choice, only strictly necessary cookies are loaded

**Consent record:**
Each choice is recorded in our system with:
- The option chosen (accept all / reject all / customized)
- Date and time of the choice
- Version of the policy in effect at the time

This record complies with GDPR audit requirements.

## 6. How to Manage Your Cookies

### From our site
You can change your choice at any time by clicking the **"Cookie Settings"** link in the site footer. This will reopen the consent banner with your current preferences.

### From your browser
You can also control cookies directly from your browser settings:

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy
- **Firefox:** Options → Privacy & Security
- **Edge:** Settings → Privacy, Search and Services

> ⚠️ **Please note:** Disabling all cookies may affect how the site functions. In particular, if you block necessary cookies, you will not be able to log in to the owner portal.

---

## 7. Cookies and Mobile Devices

If you access our site from a mobile device, cookies work in the same way. You can manage them from your device's browser settings (Chrome Mobile, Safari iOS, etc.).
